# Hardware Assembly Guide

1. Connect ESP32-S3 to breadboard.
2. Connect TCA9548A via I2C.
3. Attach MAX30102 to TCA9548A channel 0.
4. Attach MLX90640 to channel 1.
5. Attach AS7343 to channel 2.
6. Connect GSR sensor to GPIO34.
7. Connect MQ135 to GPIO35.
8. Connect OLED display to I2C bus.
9. Connect LEDs to GPIO25 and GPIO26.
10. Power system using 3.7V LiPo battery.
